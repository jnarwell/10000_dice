Rules:

To play the game please click the "roll" button. The rolls are scored as follows:

  • Single 5's - 50 points ea
  • Single 1's - 100 points ea
  • 3 of a Kind 1's - 1000 points (Can have 2 three of kinds at once since there are 6 dice)
  • 3 of a Kind 2-5 - Face value * 100 (4,4,4 is 400)
  • Straight (1,2,3,4,5,6) is worth 1500 points
